In addition to these work-flow skills, I also bring my tool bag of technical skills to the table. These include proficiencies with HTML5, CSS3, scripting in PHP, Sass, and responsive web design. This is a small snapshot of my list of ever-growing skills, for further reference see the attached resume.

Why Me?

So why am I a good fit? Well for one I'm a tinkerer, someone who can intuitively dissect an idea or technology to find what makes it tick. This mind-set has always helped me understand the inner workings of things, and aids in solving new problems. Just as in biology, tech is always evolving, and for that reason I embrace new progressive ideas and practices.
